Our story began in an apartment that became known as the Condo of Community. In a fun space filled with friends, we developed a flair for home cocktails. But it wasn't just about mixing drinks; it was about creating memorable experiences, bringing people together, and celebrating life’s special moments.

Whether you are hosting a gathering or want to explore learning the craft for yourself, our goal is to ensure that every cocktail served is enjoyed to the fullest. I started this venture not only to indulge my love for bartending, but also to foster connections among people. Let's raise a glass to unforgettable moments and impeccable service, because every occasion deserves the best. Thank you for considering Community Crafted. I look forward to serving you and helping create a memorable celebration!

Located in Southern California, we happily provide our services to LA County, Orange County, Inland Empire, and San Diego County.
